Questions?

FAQs

We’ve gathered answers to some of the questions we receive most often.

What is the dress code?

Formal Attire. Gentlemen are encouraged to wear a suit. Ladies are encouraged to wear a cocktail dress, formal dress, or elegant evening attire.

Will the ceremony and reception be indoors?

Yes. Both the ceremony at St. Ignatius Parish and the reception at The Battle House will take place indoors.

Can I take photos or videos during the ceremony?

We kindly ask that our ceremony be unplugged. Please silence your phone and keep it put away during the ceremony. We have hired a professional photographer and will be happy to share photos after the wedding.

Can I bring a plus one?

Yes. We want everyone to enjoy the celebration and are happy for guests to bring a date or plus-one.

Are children invited?

Yes, children are welcome at both the ceremony and reception.

Where should I stay?

We have reserved a room block at The Battle House Hotel for guests who would like to stay at the reception venue. Booking information will be added here soon.
